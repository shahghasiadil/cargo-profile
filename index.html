About Company

Wagner Cargo Services LLC is an upcoming brand in logistics aims to simplify logistics operation using smart technologies and future opportunities, resulting the benefits to end user in terms of quick, convenient and economical solutions.

Our operation is handled from Dubai and expanded throughout all emirates, with core areas into land freight, loading and unloading, Packing and Warehousing, air and sea freight, and custom clearances.

Vision

To provide convenient, reliable and future logistic solutions to all, thus promote growth and welfare to people and community.

Mission

To provide all logistic services conveniently with utmost commitment in delivering higher standard of solutions, transforming customer relation to inter-personalized level, and at the same time economically feasible by use of smart and technological advancements, minimalizing ecological impact.

Company Values

To strive by giving importance to human values; Compassionate, being empathetic to each other and working for a better world.
